About Prismatch

Empowering gamers to turn their passion into a career

Our Mission

Prismatch was founded with a simple goal: to create a platform where gamers of all skill levels can compete, improve, and potentially make a living doing what they love. We believe esports should be accessible to everyone, not just the elite few.

In India, where gaming is often seen just as entertainment, we're working to change perceptions and create real career opportunities in esports.
